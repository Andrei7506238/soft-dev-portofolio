Fazan Game Cheat

The app will be a secret partner to help the user master the FAZAN word game. This tool will skillfully guide through each round, suggesting clever words, destroying any possibility of opponents continuing.

FAZAN is a popular word and strategy game in Romania, often used as an educational game for learning words and developing vocabulary. The game consists of determining strings of words that are connected by the respective first and last two letters. The aim of the game is to block the opponent using words that cannot lead to other words.

This is a Flutter implementation of the original C++ Fazan Cheat featured below, with a modern and user-friendly interface. The app is designed to be easy to use and accessible to all players, providing a competitive edge in the game. The app is available for download on the Google Play Store.

Fazan Game Cheat

C++ | Qt | Graph Theory

A C++ implementation of a cheat for the classic Romanian word game, Fazan. With this program, players can gain an advantage by having access to a vast array of words at their fingertips. More often than not, the generated word will lock the next player. The program boasts a wide range of options, including toggle for word duplication, the ability to remove words said by opponents, the possibility to play with custom words and more.

Built on a custom directed multigraph data structure and a greedy algorithm, this program guarantees lightning-fast speed even with a massive set of words, including all Scrabble-accepted words in Romanian.

Not only is this software a great tool for gaining a decisive edge in the game, but it can also be used to generate the complete list of words used for blocking. Fun fact: you can link at least 49047 words in a single game of Fazan without closing off options for the next player or repeating a word.

Note: This is a solo personal project.

OpenGl Fish Aquarium

C++ | OpenGl | 3D
